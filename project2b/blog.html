Blog Page — Details

Purpose

The blog page will show articles related to parkour written by ParkourATL admins and affiliates. It will also show tutorials, so the articles might be accompanied by videos explaining the technique and movements in a more detailed and realistic manner. After clicking on a post, the user will be taken to a separate page with the post itself. Blog posts will have the option of receiving comments, too.

Web items that might apply to this website

  • Hyperlinks to all other pages within this site
  • Hyperlinks to third-party websites
  • Images
  • Video
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • AJAX calls
